Compliance Compliant_at Story Runner YAML

Verify an Asset against its compliance policies.

asset_label is required from a previously created asset. The asset_id is retrieved and inserted as the first argument to compliance.compliant_at().

Setting report: true will trigger a report to be printed on the compliance status.

---
steps:
  - step:
      action: COMPLIANCE_COMPLIANT_AT
      description: Check Compliance of EV pump 1.
      report: true
      asset_label: ev pump 1
